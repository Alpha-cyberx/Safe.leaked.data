COLLECTED BY CYBERXCODE – Types of Cyber Crimes with Misused Documents (2025)

Official Sources & Proof Links Included

1. Digital Arrest Scam

Misused Documents/Details: Aadhaar number, PAN, bank account details, OTPs, phone number (for video calls).

How it Works: Scammers pose as police/CBI, claim your Aadhaar/PAN is linked to crime, demand payments for "verification". Losses: ₹210 crore in 2025.

Sources:
cybercrime.gov.in – I4C Official Portal
Forbes India Explainer
Bloomberg Report

2. Identity Theft & Impersonation

Misused Documents/Details: Aadhaar, PAN, bank info, social media/email accounts, phone numbers.

How it Works: Stolen data used to hijack accounts, solicit money from contacts, or create fake profiles. 75% of cyber crimes involve this.

Sources:
cybercrime.gov.in – Identity Theft Reporting
Lawpret Comprehensive Report
CERT-In Guidelines (IT Act 66C/66D)

3. PAN 2.0 Phishing Scam

Misused Documents/Details: PAN, Aadhaar, bank details, OTPs (via fake upgrade emails).

How it Works: Fake emails claim PAN upgrade, steal details for account takeover. Surged in 2025 with AI fakes.

Sources:
Times of India Warning
UIDAI Phishing Alerts

4. Fake KYC & Synthetic Identity Fraud

Misused Documents/Details: Aadhaar, PAN, birth certificates, QR codes (AI-generated fakes).

How it Works: 1,800+ fake websites generate counterfeit IDs for loans/BNPL/money laundering. 450% rise since 2022.

Sources:
CNBC TV18 Investigation
DSCI Report

5. Aadhaar Data Breach & e-KYC Fraud

Misused Documents/Details: Aadhaar numbers, fingerprints, PAN, passports (from leaks like 815 million PII breach).

How it Works: Leaked data sold on dark web for fake e-KYC/loans/SIM swaps.

Sources:
The Hindu Explainer
UIDAI Official Response
Bitdefender Report

6. OTP & SIM Swap Fraud

Misused Documents/Details: Phone numbers, OTPs, Aadhaar for SIM duplication.

How it Works: Fake calls steal OTPs; 9.42 lakh SIMs blocked in 2025.

Sources:
Seek4S Guide
DoT/PIB Report

7. Phishing & Investment Scams

Misused Documents/Details: Email IDs, PAN/Aadhaar, bank details (via fake apps).

How it Works: Fake promises of high returns; ₹1.2 lakh crore projected loss in 2025.

Sources:
Drishti IAS Analysis
I4C Awareness Portal

8. Fake Aadhaar/PAN Printing Fraud

Misused Documents/Details: Customer-submitted Aadhaar/PAN scans.

How it Works: 167,000+ fake IDs generated via cyber cafes.

Sources:
The Hindu Report
Business Connect Magazine

9. Ransomware & Data Breaches

Misused Documents/Details: Aadhaar, emails, addresses (41 million exposed in Hathway breach).

How it Works: Vulnerabilities lead to leaks; data sold for identity theft.

Sources:
63SATS Cybertech Blog
Corbado Blog

10. AI-Powered Synthetic ID Fraud

Misused Documents/Details: AI-generated PAN/Aadhaar stitched with real data.

How it Works: 10,000+ fakes/month; 300% surge since 2023.

Jisa Softech Report
